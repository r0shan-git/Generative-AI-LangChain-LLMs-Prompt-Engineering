1.Inception is a 2010 science fiction action thriller film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by entering people's dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by performing the impossible task of planting an idea into someone's subconscious. The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Elliot Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, and Cillian Murphy.
2.Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is a fantasy film released in 2001. Directed by Chris Columbus, it follows Harry Potter, a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The movie stars Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson, Rupert Grint, Richard Harris, Maggie Smith, and Alan Rickman.
3.I recently watched Interstellar, and it completely blew my mind. Christopher Nolan directed the movie, which came out in 2014. It stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, Michael Caine, and Matt Damon. The film is a mix of Science Fiction, Adventure, and Drama, following astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity.
